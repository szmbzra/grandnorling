About US

Welcome To grand Norling Hotel's Resort

Beautiful picnic spots and wedding venues

Welcome to Grand Norling Hotel’s Resort, where nature’s beauty meets unparalleled luxury in the heart of Nepal. Here, every moment is an invitation to experience a destination that blends cultural richness with modern elegance, offering you a journey like no other.

Set amidst the revered UNESCO World Heritage Sites, Grand Norling is a tribute to Nepal’s timeless heritage. Our resort offers a serene escape surrounded by the sacred history of the Bajrayogini Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, and Pashupatinath Temple, all while overlooking the majestic Gauri Shankar Himal range. From our rooftop terrace to the lush gardens, you'll be captivated by stunning views that connect you to the natural wonders around you.

At Grand Norling, every detail is crafted with your relaxation in mind. Perfect your golf swing on our pristine course or enjoy the wildlife that roams freely across our peaceful estate. Our commitment to providing exceptional comfort and joy ensures that your stay is not just a retreat, but a memorable experience.

We invite you to indulge in the luxury and tranquility of Grand Norling Hotel’s Resort. More than just a stay, it's a journey into opulence, designed to awaken your senses and leave you with unforgettable memories. Discover the true essence of hospitality and make Grand Norling part of your story—where every moment is extraordinary.
